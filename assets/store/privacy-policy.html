Pixel Forge — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

Developer: The Cochran Block LLC

Data Collection

Pixel Forge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All sprite generation happens locally on your device using embedded AI models. No data leaves your phone.

Network Access

Pixel Forge does not require an internet connection. The app works entirely offline. No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting is sent anywhere.

Permissions

Pixel Forge requests no special device permissions. It does not access your camera, contacts, location, microphone, or any other sensor.

Third-Party Services

Pixel Forge uses no third-party SDKs, ad networks, or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

Pixel Forge is suitable for all ages. It collects no data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
